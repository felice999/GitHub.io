New: "Learning Language Chats" available now!

29 Mar 2025 3:08 PM | Anonymous member (Administrator)

Learn a Language Your Way – Pay What You Want!

The Club is excited to introduce "Learning Language Chats" – a revolutionary mentoring program designed to help learners of English, French, German, Italian, and Spanish master their target language without breaking the bank.

Why Choose Learning Language Chats?

✅ High-Standard, Structured 1-on-1 Lessons – Personalized, in-person chats (lessons) tailored to your goals.
✅ Pay What You Want – You decide the value of your learning experience.
✅ Long-Term Study Planning – Expert guidance on structuring your language journey, including:

  • The best mix of online & in-person studies

  • Recommendations for top-rated language schools (when, where, duration, and cost)

  • Customized strategies for efficient progress

This program is perfect for learners who want affordable, high-quality mentoring while optimizing their study plan for fluency.
